About Us

The Barik Nagar Iccha Foundation is a grassroots nonprofit dedicated to uplifting the people of Barik Nagar through education, healthcare, and social empowerment. We believe in the power of community-driven change and work tirelessly to bring sustainable development to our region.

Our Projects

  • Free Health Camps and Medical Aid
  • Education Programs for Underprivileged Children
  • Women Empowerment and Skill Development
  • Clean Drinking Water Initiatives
